Welcome to RetroHack!

We are a fun, beginner-friendly platform to join virtual hackathons and coding competitions.

About Us

Retrohack is tinder of hackathons RetroHack is made for young coders and hobbyists who want to learn and compete in a playful, colorful environment. We focus on fun, creativity, and learning by doing!

Benefits of Joining

  • Learn coding through hands-on challenges
  • Compete with friends and community members
  • Improve problem-solving and teamwork skills
  • Earn badges and recognition on the leaderboard
  • Fun, colorful, and easy-to-use platform for all ages
